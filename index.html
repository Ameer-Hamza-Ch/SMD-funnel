How the process Works?

From files submission to final ESD-packaged delivery — three simple steps to get your boards assembled.

Send Details & Get Quote

Submit your Gerber files, BOM list, and batch size requirements. Our engineers do a swift DFM review and send an itemised quote within 12h.

Procurement & Assembly

We procure missing components from trusted distributors, load Siemens SIPLACE lines, apply solder paste, and perform reflow soldering.

Testing & Delivery

We run every single assembled board through our high-definition AOI scanner, inspect complex BGAs, package in ESD-safe bags, and ship.
